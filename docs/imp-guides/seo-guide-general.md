## 7. Recommended New Pages & Content

### 7A. Condition-Specific Landing Pages

Each page should contain 800–1,200 words of original content following this structure:

**Page template structure:**

1. **H1** — Condition + location keyword
2. **Empathetic opening** — 150 words validating the reader's experience
3. **H2: Signs & Symptoms section** — 200 words identifying when to seek help
4. **H2: How Therapy Helps** — 200 words on the therapeutic approach
5. **H2: Specific Modalities Used** — 150 words on CBT, EFT, etc. as relevant
6. **H2: What to Expect** — 100 words on the session experience
7. **H2: FAQ section** — 3–5 questions with FAQPage schema markup
8. **CTA block** — "Book a Free Consultation" with phone number

**Pages to create (priority order):**

1. `/anxiety-therapy` — Highest local search volume
2. `/couples-therapy` — High commercial intent
3. `/depression-therapy` — Second-highest condition search volume
4. `/teen-therapy` — Underserved by competitors in EDH
5. `/online-therapy-california` — Captures statewide telehealth searches
6. `/family-therapy` — Aligns with existing AMFT credential
7. `/grief-therapy` — Low competition locally
8. `/life-transitions` — Differentiator keyword
9. `/self-esteem-therapy` — Long-tail, low competition
10. `/divorce-therapy` — High emotional intent
